...LET THE CURRENT-SEA TAKE YOU

THIS 9 FOOT TALL, 60'S LONGBOARD WAS TRANSFORMED INTO A MIXED MEDIUM CANVAS. THE TOP AND MIDDLE OF THE BOARD WAS METICULOUSLY HAND CARVED TO A DEPTH OF 1/2"-3/4" AND THEN EPOXY SEALED TO CREATE A 3D RELIEF OF CHE QUEVARA, THE ISLAND OF CUBA AND THE RED GLOWING EMBERS OF THE CIGAR ASH. THE BOARD IS SELECTIVELY WRAPPED IN REAL COHIBA CIGAR LEAVES. THE "CIGAR" WRAPPER IS COMPRISED OF ORIGINAL 1960'S CUBAN CURRENCY, WITH CHE QUEVARA'S SIGNATURE AS THE PRESIDENT OF THE CUBAN NATIONAL BANK. THIS ORIGINAL CUBAN CURRENCY IS BORDERED ON THE TOP AND BOTTOM BY ORIGINAL, VINTAGE CIGAR BAND WRAPPERS FROM PROMINENT CUBAN CIGAR MANUFACTURERS IN THE 1960S.

CALIFORNIA SURF ANTHOLOGY

THIS SHORT STICK IS A CALIFORNIA HISTORICAL SURF ANTHOLOGY. THE GOLDEN STATE BEAR IS CUT FROM THE FLAG AND ITS BACK IS CAREFULLY SHAPED TO MIRROR THE CALIFORNIA COAST LINE, HIGHLIGHTING FAMOUS SURF SPOTS.
THE COLLAGED IMAGES DEPICT THE FAMOUS SURFERS THAT HELPED SHAPE CALIFORNIA SURFING AND ARE LAID OUT NEAR THE SURF SPOTS THEY DOMINATED.
A SIMPLE BAND OF US $1 BILLS FRAME THE US FLAG AT THE BOARD'S TAIL, WHILE A MIGHTY SEQUOIA TREE ANOINTS THE BOARD'S TIP.
